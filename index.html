Menu

Scrambled Egg Sandwich

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs (usually chicken eggs) stirred or beaten together in a pan while being gently heated, typically with salt, butter and various other ingredients.

Avocado Toast With Egg

Avocado toast is a type of open sandwich made with mashed avocado and salt, pepper. Potential additional ingredients that enhance the flavor are olive oil, hummus, red pepper flakes and many other toppings.

Crispy Fruit Sandwich

Fruit sandwich is vegetarian sandwich consisting of consists of fruit filling between bread. The most commonly used fruits are pineapple, banana and apple.

Savory Oatmeal

Oatmeal is a type of coarse flour made of hulled oat grains. In a 100 gram amount, cooked oatmeal provides 71 Calories and contains 29% of the Daily Value for manganese and moderate content of phosphorus and zinc.

PB&J Waffle

A waffle is a dish made from leavened batter or dough that is cooked between two plates that are patterned to give a characteristic size, shape, and surface impression. Waffles are eaten throughout the world, has over a dozen regional varieties.

Pesto Pasta

Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine. Pasta is typically made from an unleavened dough of durum wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or various shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Grilled Steak Tacos

Taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn or wheat tortilla folded or rolled around a filling. A taco can be made with a variety of fillings, including beef, pork, chicken, seafood, vegetables, and cheese.

Chicken Bites

A chicken nugget is a chicken product made from chicken meat that is breaded or battered, then deep-fried or baked. Fast food restaurants usually fry their nuggets in vegetable oil but we serve backed.

Avocado Beans

Avocado Beans is very nutritional dish high in protein, complex carbohydrates, folate, and iron. A typical serving is moderate to rich in several B vitamins, vitamin K and E with good content of vitamin C, vitamin E and potassium.

Basil Cheese

Filled with goodness of basil and all the nutitions of cheese, this tasty dish surely deserve a try. You will surely appriciate our trademark dish.

Chicken Biriyani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish with its origins from the Indian subcontinent.This dish is especially popular throughout the Indian subcontinent, as well as among the diaspora from the region.

Asparagus Barbecue

Coocked in traditional styled baebecue and served with aspargus. The technique which we use involves cooking using smoke at low temperatures and long cooking times (several hours).

Crisp Cucumber

Crisp garlic breads and loaded with a lot fresh cucumber with other vegetables. Along with good taste it provides 4 percent of your daily potassium, 3 percent of your daily fiber and 4 percent of your daily vitamin C.

Basil Cook

Basil cook is a Provençal cold sauce made from cloves of garlic, fresh basil, and olive oil. It is somewhat similar to the Ligurian sauce pesto, although it lacks pine nuts. Other versions of the recipe include grated parmesan, pecorino, or similar hard cheeses.

Delicious Crust

Delicious crust is pizza prepared according to several different styles developed in Chicago. The pan in which it is baked gives the pizza its characteristically high edge which provides ample space for large amounts of cheese and a chunky tomato sauce.

Bluberry Chocolate Cake

Bluberry Chocolate Cake consists of several layers of chocolate sponge cake sandwiched with whipped cream and cherries. It is decorated with additional whipped cream, maraschino cherries, and chocolate shavings.

Choco Muffins

A deliciously sweet muffin recipe that makes an excellent breakfast. We provide different vesrions of these tasty muffins select the best one for yourself.

Fudgy Chocolate Cake

A Fudgy Chocolate cake is a chocolate cake containing fudge. Variations include pudding fudge cake, made with chocolate cake mix, chocolate pudding, and chocolate chips. When made with additional chocolate, the recipe is sometimes known as "Death by Chocolate".

Date Swirl Cookies

A Date Swirl cookie is a baked or cooked food that is small, flat and sweet. It usually contains flour, sugar and some type of oil or fat. It also includes date pudding along with nuts.

Fudge Nut Cookies

Fudge nut cookies are extra nut cookies baked in low temperature and for longer period which provides its unique and crispy taste.
